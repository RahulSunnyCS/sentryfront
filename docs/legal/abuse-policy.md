## Overview

VibeSafe is a security scanning tool intended for authorized testing of websites you own or have permission to test. This policy defines prohibited uses, abuse reporting procedures, and contact information.

---

## Prohibited Uses

### 1. Unauthorized Scanning

❌ **DO NOT** scan websites you do not own or operate without explicit written permission.

Examples of prohibited scanning:
- Scanning competitors' websites to find vulnerabilities for malicious purposes
- Scanning government websites without authorization
- Scanning financial institutions, healthcare systems, or critical infrastructure
- Scanning any website that explicitly prohibits automated testing

### 2. Circumventing Rate Limits

❌ **DO NOT** attempt to bypass rate limits through:
- Distributed scanning from multiple IP addresses
- Using VPNs or proxies to create multiple accounts
- Scripted automated scanning beyond allowed quotas

### 3. Malicious Use

❌ **DO NOT** use VibeSafe to:
- Identify vulnerabilities for exploitation or blackmail
- Publicly disclose findings to harm a website's reputation without responsible disclosure
- Resell or redistribute scan reports as a competing service
- Scan websites to gather competitive intelligence unrelated to security

### 4. Service Disruption

❌ **DO NOT**:
- Overload our infrastructure with excessive requests
- Attempt to reverse-engineer or circumvent security controls
- Interfere with other users' scans or reports

---

## Consequences of Abuse

Violations may result in:

1. **Immediate account suspension** (if auth is enabled)
2. **IP address blocking** (permanent or temporary)
3. **Report to law enforcement** (for illegal activity)
4. **Legal action** (for damages or violations of CFAA/GDPR/other laws)
5. **Notification to affected website owners** (if unauthorized scanning detected)

## Abuse Reporting

### If You Received an Unauthorized Scan

If you are a website owner and received a scan from VibeSafe without authorization:

**Email**: abuse@[DOMAIN].com

**Include**:
- Your website URL
- Date and time of the scan (check server logs)
- IP address of the scanner (if available)
- Evidence that scanning was unauthorized (e.g., robots.txt prohibition, lack of permission)

**Response Time**: Within 48 hours

**Actions We Will Take**:
1. Investigate the scan request (check requester IP, account, timestamp)
2. Suspend the offending account (if applicable)
3. Provide you with available information about the scan initiator (subject to privacy laws)
4. Block future scans from that IP/account
5. Report to law enforcement if criminal activity is suspected

## DMCA and Copyright Claims

If you believe VibeSafe scan reports contain copyrighted material that was used without authorization:

**Email**: legal@[DOMAIN].com

**Include**:
- Description of the copyrighted work
- URL of the scan report in question
- Your contact information and copyright ownership proof
- Statement of good faith belief that use is unauthorized

**Process**:
1. We will review within 5 business days
2. If valid, we will remove the infringing content
3. Notify the scan initiator of the takedown
4. Allow counter-notice period (10 days)
