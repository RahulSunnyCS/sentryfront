## 💡 Why Compliance Matters

### Legal & Financial Impact

**GDPR (Europe):**
- Average fine: €877,000 (DLA Piper 2023)
- Max fine: €20M or 4% global revenue
- 1,600+ fines issued to date

**CCPA (California):**
- $2,500 per unintentional violation
- $7,500 per intentional violation
- Private right of action: $100-$750 per consumer

**ADA (Accessibility):**
- 4,600+ lawsuits filed in 2023
- Average settlement: $10,000-$50,000
- 97% of websites fail WCAG 2.2 AA

### Business Impact

**For Startups:**
- Required for enterprise sales (compliance checklist)
- Necessary for investor due diligence
- Enables EU/California market expansion
- Reduces legal risk

**For Enterprise:**
- SOC 2 compliance requirement
- GDPR/CCPA mandate for customer data
- WCAG 2.2 AA for accessibility lawsuits
- PCI-DSS for payment processing
