===========================================
HOW TO ADD IN VERCEL
===========================================

1. In Vercel import screen, scroll to "Environment Variables"
2. For each variable above:
   - Click "Add New"
   - Name: <variable-name>
   - Value: <variable-value>
   - Select: "All Environments"
   - Click "Add"
3. After adding all variables, click "Deploy"
