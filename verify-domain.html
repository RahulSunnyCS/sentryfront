Required for Active Testing

Verify you own this domain

To run active security tests on taskflow.app, we need proof you own or are authorized to test it.

⚖️
Why verify? Active testing sends real attack probes to your site (SQL injection, XSS, fuzzing). Verification is your legal authorization — it protects both you and us under the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (US) and equivalent laws globally. Without it, scanning is potentially illegal. With it, you're explicitly testing your own property.
🔧
DNS TXT Record Recommended
~60 seconds · most reliable
1. Add this TXT record in your DNS panel:
Type TXT
Name @
Value vibesafe-verify=a7f3c2e1d4b8
2. Wait 1–5 minutes for DNS propagation
Common DNS panels: Cloudflare, Namecheap, GoDaddy, Route 53, Google Domains
